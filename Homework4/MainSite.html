Contrast, Repetition, Alignment, and Proximity

By Justin Hoang

Contrast:

Three Sided Cube

  • This site has simple shapes easily identifying what's on screen
  • The colors are distinct from each other, making the shapes pop out from the background
  • Moving from one panel to another using both a different yet consistent palette for their website

Repetition:

Obeymayer

  1. Its repetition when searching for information off the side of the website is consistent
  2. It's simplicity remains consistent and easier to track
  3. They use similar colors for the webiste, following an easy to understand theme

Alignment:

The Design Corner: Alignment

REASON #1
The alignment within the website is easy to follow and find

REASON #2
Different pieces of information is separate from one another

REASON #3
It even uses itself as an example of using Alignment in Web Design

Proximity:

Wellington City Council

  1. The site provides appropriate spacing in its descriptions
  2. Can easily find the information it provides
  3. Information is spaced enough from the others to have it stand out
